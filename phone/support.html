At Swecque Studios, we value your (the user’s) data, containing gameplay information, comments, suggestions or feedback. Our Privacy Policy sets out how we collect, use and disclose “personal information” and other “non personal information” we have gathered about you.

What Personal Information do we collect?

“Personal Information” is information or an opinion that identifies you as an individual.

We may collect and store information such as your (user)name and email if you contact us directly, if you submit a review or comment on platforms such as the Google Play Store or post a message relating to us on social media or any other public, online platform. We do not collect any other Personal Information right now, not even ingame activities or progress. Although this may change in the future.

Sensitive Information

Sensitive information is defined in the US Privacy Act (1974) to include information or opinion about such things as an individual’s racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, membership of a political association, religious or philosophical beliefs, membership of a professional body, criminal records or health information. Swecque Studios will not attempt to obtain nor record any sensitive information.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We will not sell, trade, rent or disclose any of the personal information you provide to us for any reason, without your prior consent. Otherwise, your Personal Information will only be disclosed where required or allowable by law.

Security of Personal Information

Your Personal Information is stored offsite in a manner that reasonably protects it from misuse, loss, unauthorized access, modification or disclosure. We don’t guarantee website links or policy of authorised third parties and you access those sites at your own risk.

Contact and support

If you have any questions or complaints about our Privacy Policy, you can fill in the contactform below in the section Contact us, or send your message directly to:

contact@swecquestudios.com

Access to your Personal Information

Under the Privacy Act, NPP 6 gives you the right to access the Personal Information we hold about you and to update and/or correct it, subject to certain exceptions. If you wish to access your Personal Information, please make out your request to us in writing at the address listed below. Swecque Studios will not charge any fee for your access request, but may charge an administrative fee. Please be aware that in order to protect the Personal Information in our database, we will require identification from you before releasing any information.

What other information do we collect?

Other “Non Personal Information” such as device specific information is been processed and collected via the Google Play Store. We have insight in this information in a completely anonymous way. For more information, we refer you to Google’s privacy policy:

https://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/

Currently Swecque Studios does not use any advertising, cookies or monetizing system. Neither is any other Third Party involved but Google. This may change in the future.

Policy updates

This Policy may change from time to time. The most current version of our Privacy Policy can be found at:

https://swecquestudios.com/support.html#privacy

We will always keep this up to date so that you know what information we will collect from you, how we may use it, and when we may disclose it.

Q&A's

Does Swecque Studios have any games or other products besides 'Marvin: late for School'?

No. We just started this thing and for now we only want to focus on Marvin: Late for School. We cannot say if there will be more games coming. There are, however, plans for merchandising products, like Swecque-caps and Swecque-shirts. Let us know if you're interested!

Is this all serious? Because... I mean... just 24 downloads?

Yes. This has become quite serious. And even the big apps with millions of downloads must have been at 24 once. It's now up to you to make it 25!

How do you make money? Because the game is free, there are no ads and no in-app purchases.

Very clever question, if I may say so myself. For the answer I would like to refer you to this quote:

It is not about money,

it is about Swecque!

Is Marvin: Late for School coming to other platforms? Like iOS or Windows Phone?

Well, this is a tricky one. Because expanding to other platforms does not really fit in our vision from the previous question. In other words, it requires some money to bring the game to those platforms.

Contact us

If you have a question, a complaint, want to submit a review or just need to share a story, feel free to contact us by mail on contact@swecquestudios.com

Report a bug

We are working hard to solve the most frustrating bugs. Although it's technically impossible to make a game completely bug-free. Our focus is primarily on gameplay and experience, so only if you find a bug that's interrupting the gameplay our letting down your experience, then tell us on contact@swequestudios.com with subject: BUGREPORT
